MISSION

As many of the patients that find their way to us do so as a matter of last hope the main goal of the establishment is crystal clear. Many of the regular clinics and hospitals of our time cannot - despite their excellent capabilities in other areas - provide efficient treatment for some cases. With our set of excellent therapies and the profound understanding of chronic, bacterial diseases of Dr. Julius Hellenthal MD often we can, and it is our mission to do so, for every patient deserves quality of life.


Of course we also care about the poor of Nicaragua which for complicated diseases completely lack access to any treatment. With us a certain percentage will get treatment they could never dream of under normal circumstances for free. For the future our last big goal is to create over 1000 work posts for Nicaraguan people as the clinic and its services expand.
